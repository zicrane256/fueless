FueLess 1.0

FueLess App is a multifunctional e-fueling mobile powered platform. The product is aimed at exploiting the power of numbers to improve the bargaining power for petroleum products by automobile users in Africa. This is a product of Firm Environmental Engineering Consults Ltd.

FueLess strives to be the e-power to power B2B in the largely self price rule by powerful multinational petroleum energy companies

The App also helps Government and organizations to close fuel licks by ensuring that fuel allocations to institutional fleets only serves intended purpose on specified automobile. The electronic identifier on the car makes it hard for switching since the traditional fuel cards can be shared. Engage FueLess, sit and watch your expenses on petroleum products drop every day!
